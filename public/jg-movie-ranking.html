The Best Movies According to Jim

My top 4 movies of all-time.

Saving Private Ryan

This is my favourite war movie, a moving and harrowing interpretation of the second world war.

The Return of the Jedi

This is one of my top sci-fi movies, a classic ending to the Star Wars saga.

An American Werewolf in London

One of the scariest films I ever saw as a child, to this day the scene on the moors is still chilling to watch.

Spiderman: Into the Spider-Verse

An incredible piece of animation work and a well told story. An amazing animated film and a true visual feast.
